Lasagna

Description

This has all been stolen wholesale from John Chandler at Allrecipes.com

This is a filling and heart warming lasagna. The ragu being made with both Italian sausage and lean ground beef adds additional depth.

Being made with ricotta instead of bechamel this is an American-Italian lasagna instead of the classic Italian. It is very scrumptious and all homecooks should know how to cook both styles.

Ingredients

  • Meat: Sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic: 1 onion 2 cloves of garlic
  • Tomato Products: 1 can of chrushed tomatoes, 2 cans of premade tomato sauce and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Add two tablespoons of sugar to enhance the flavour.
  • Spices and Seasonings: Fresh parsley, dried baisl leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: If you feel fancy you can make your own otherwise storebought is fine.
  • Cheese: You need 3 cheeses for this badboy. Parmesan, Mozzarella and ricotta cheese.
  • Egg: One egg to help bind the ricotta.

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest
  7. Enjoy!
