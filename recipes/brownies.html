QTs Brownies

Description

This has all been stolen wholesale from QTCinderella at Youtube

Alot of bang for your buck in this one.

Ingredients

  • Unsalted Butter: 113g
  • Oil: 1 tablespoon
  • Brown Sugar: 100g
  • Sugar: 134g
  • Eggs: 2
  • Flour: 68g
  • Cocoa: 50g
  • Salt: 1/4 teaspoon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175 celsius
  2. Line or oil a 9x9 baking tray
  3. Mix dry ingredients:
  4. Flour, Salt and Cocoa
  5. Soften butter in microwave(cover the bowl or clean the microwave)
  6. Mix Wet Ingredients
  7. Butter, Oil, Brown sugar, Regular sugar and Eggs
  8. Combine wet and dry ingredients
  9. Pour into the prepared tray and tap out air bubbles
  10. Bake for 20 mins check if it is still jiggly. If jiggly give 5 more mins then check again.
