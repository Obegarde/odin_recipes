Carbonara

Description

The picture was stolen from the BBC

Ingredients

  • Dried Spaghetti: 225g
  • Salt: For salting the pasta water, don't be shy with it.
  • Bacon: 100g
  • Eggs: 1
  • Black Pepper: Freshly ground
  • Parmesan: Freshly grated, maybe 50g. I dunno. Judge it yourself.

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil.
  2. Add salt to the water, you want it salty.
  3. Boil Spaghetti until slighty less done than you want it.
  4. Slice the bacon into small pieces
  5. Save a cup of the water.
  6. Drain the pasta.
  7. Fry the bacon until crispy.
  8. Mix the parmesan, black pepper and the egg. While mixing add half a cup of the hot water to temp the mixture.
  9. Add the pasta to the fried bacon, add the rest of the pasta water and bring it to a boil again.
  10. Take it off the heat, add the egg mixture, control the temp and keep it all moving until the sauce has thickened without scrambling.
  11. Take off the heat and cover until you have gotten plates out. Serve and Enjoy
